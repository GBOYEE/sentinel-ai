# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respectful disagreement
- Graceful acceptance of constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior:
- Personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination
- Publishing private information without permission
- Other unprofessional conduct

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the maintainers at Oyebanjiadegboyee@gmail.com. Maintainers reserve the right to remove or edit comments, commits, code, or ban users as needed.
